How It Works

From raw content to intelligent answers in four steps

1

Ingest

Upload documents, paste URLs, or submit raw text through the unified ingestion endpoint

2

Chunk

Content is split into ~512 token chunks with overlap for optimal retrieval

3

Embed

Generate vector embeddings using Ollama's nomic-embed-text model

4

Query

Search semantically and get AI-generated answers from your knowledge
